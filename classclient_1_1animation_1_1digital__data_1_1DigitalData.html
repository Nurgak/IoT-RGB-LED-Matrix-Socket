Detailed Description

Digital data animation class.

Animation showing:

  • Time in hours, minutes and seconds in 24h format.
  • Month, with 3 letters.
  • Day of the month, with 2 digits.
  • Year, with 4 digits.
  • Day of the week, with 3 letters.
  • Temperature, in degrees Celcius
  • Relative humidity, in percent.
  • Air quality index (AQI), in qualitative 5-level system.

To fit all this data on the 32x32 display a very small font is used: a 5x3 pixel font, which is the smallest that remains easily readable.

To configure the animation for local data pass the relevant keyword arguments:

  • key OpenWeatherMap API key.
  • city The city for which the temperature, humidy and AQI will be requested.
  • timezone Time timezone for which the time will be displayed.
